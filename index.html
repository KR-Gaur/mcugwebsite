Teknothon 2k17 is here!

Teknothon is an event organized by MIT Computer Users Group.
The theme this year is Data Science.

As a part of Teknothon, there will be various sessions, hands-on workshops on data analytics & neural networks and The Reverse Interview by industry experts.

"How stuff works?" will give you an in on the working of some latest stuff like bitcoins, Snapchat, bio-metric scanners and much more.
